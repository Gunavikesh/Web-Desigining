Target 100

MultArt has set a pledge to reach 100 colleges as one of its milestones. We are committed not only to discover talents in the students from those college students, but also to create a platform to nurture them. Target 100 is the beginning of the exploring the sky beyond the horizon and MulteArts is getting ready for a long journey.

Student Talent

Everyone is born with some talent. It just requires to be realized, nurtured and performed on. A young mind is the strongest incubation for any talent. Hence MulteArts aims to find talents in the students and help to nurture them at the early stage to shine it the most. MulteArts is a platform to gather experts from various fields who can mentor the students to excel in their area.

Chef Master

“Eating is a necessity, but cooking is an art”. MulteArt admires those artists and appreciate their art on a grand platform. Chef master is an initiative from MulteArts to promote the passionate chefs and their skills through different social media platforms and events.

Meet and greet

MulteArts is determined to try out all kinds of possibilities and ways to reach talents and bring them to the platform. Hence, experts from MulteArts conduct free "meet and greet" sessions for students in schools and colleges. Through this program experts speak to students, awaken them about the importance of their skills and talent and educate them about different programs available for them on MulteArts platforms. It happens to be a great way to meet more and more students and help them in finding their gem.
