Guest Lecture

MulteArts is a hub of experts from different fields and skills. The expert team connects with instructions as guest lecture and conduct various sessions designed for 360 degree development of a student. Apart from skill development and talent search, different sessions for softskill and personality development, such as leadership skill, time management, memory management, presentation skill, communication skill etc. are also being provided.

Third party integration

Divided we fall, United we Rule. MulteArts aims to reach more and more experts to associate with students with enormous amount of expertise. Induction

The first time experience has been playing a role of a magic stick for building relationship either from people to people or people in organizations. MulteArts is passionate about helping individuals to connect with new organizations being more comfortable and close. The multeArts induction programs get customized based on the type of audience, type of institution and objective of the on board activity.
