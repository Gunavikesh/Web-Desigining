Who We Are

Multe Arts is a unique company in its own way. We are here with a purpose to nourish and educate the masses about the countless opportunities that lie within reach. Our concept is to carve the hidden treasure of talent in an individual and turning them into a Versatile.

Our organization aims to uplift an individual with multiple skills to sustain and standalone in this cut-throat competitive world. To reach this pinnacle we enhanced the trajectory of quality learning through courses such as Moksha, college to Corporate, The power of Mind, Hidden Talents, The magic of memory, LRM skills, Graphology, Nidhi-the money management, Vyakthitv-the personality development program, Vikas-the interview skills program and many more are in the pipeline.

Women empowerment and welfare of the vulnerable form the core concepts of the themes initiated by our organization. Multe Arts launched the SHAKTHI program under which Ms. Veena trained more than 1000 women across India. Multe Arts intends to spread its wings nationwide in the coming years.
